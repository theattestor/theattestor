Frequently Asked Questions

What services does The Attestor provide?

We connect clients with trusted notaries and field agents who deliver mobile notarizations, apostille assistance, vehicle and property inspections, document witnessing, and customized photo or video verification services.

How much does a notary charge?

Fees vary by state. For example, in Ohio the maximum fee per notarial act is $5 per signature. Travel or service specific fees may apply and are disclosed up front.

Can you notarize documents remotely?

Where state law allows Remote Online Notarization, we can complete notarizations online. If not permitted in your state, we dispatch a mobile notary to your location.

Do you provide apostille services?

Yes. We prepare and route eligible documents for apostille or state certification for international use under The Hague Convention and we provide clear status updates.

Can you inspect a vehicle or property before I buy or rent it?

Yes. Agents capture timestamped photos, video when requested, and a condition checklist for vehicles, real estate, and short term rentals so you can decide with confidence.

How do you prevent fraud in your services?

The Attestor follows strict state and federal protocols. Every assignment includes ID verification before any notarization or attestation, GPS and timestamp confirmation, and secure delivery of reports. These steps help ensure authenticity and protect clients from tampering or misrepresentation.

Do your agents travel outside big cities?

Yes. We are growing a nationwide network that includes metro, suburban, and many rural areas. Some assignments may include travel fees, disclosed in advance.

Is there a cost for agents to join The Attestor Network?

No. Joining is free. We match opportunities to your location and availability, provide clear job briefs, and pay per assignment. No gimmicks.

What if I need a service not listed?

We offer customizable solutions. If it requires a trustworthy neutral party to document or verify, tell us what you need and we will tailor the assignment.
